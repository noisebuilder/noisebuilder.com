About

Raw energy.
Underground legacy.

Noisebuilder is a founder member of the legendary French rave collective Heretik System.

Active since the underground free party scene of the 1990s, he became known for his powerful hardtek productions and explosive DJ sets across Europe.

With a raw, direct and uncompromising sound, Noisebuilder remains a key figure of the European rave movement.

Today he continues to perform selected DJ sets and share music while preserving the original spirit of the underground.
